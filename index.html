About Me

My name is Zhangir Yestayev, and I am a student with a deep commitment to academics and competitive sports. These studies have sharpened my analytical skills and strengthened my interest in pursuing applied mathematics and systems analysis as future career paths. I enjoy tackling complex problems, learning new concepts, and applying knowledge in ways that connect theory with real‑world solutions.

These experiences, along with my involvement in soccer, have taught me how to perform under pressure, work effectively with others, and remain consistent in pursuit of growth. I strive to combine intellectual rigor with athletic discipline, preparing myself to make meaningful contributions in both academic and professional settings.
